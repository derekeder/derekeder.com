Electrifying Our Old Oak Park Home

In 2022 and 2023, I documented our process of fully electrifying our 100-year-old home in Oak Park, IL. We installed solar panels on our roof (giving us a source of free renewable electricity), switched out our gas furnace with electric heat pumps, and replaced all gas appliances with electric equivalents.

Home electrification is one of the most impactful actions any household can do to fight climate change, and it will improve your home comfort and lower your energy bills in the process. And with all the new incentives and rebates available, it costs less than you think.
